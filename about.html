About Us

What is AffiStrike?

AffiStrike is a simple, AI-powered tool built to answer one fundamental question for every entrepreneur and creator: "How is my business or product idea?"

Before you spend time, money, or energy building a product, you need a quick, objective "gut check." AffiStrike acts as your digital sounding board—analyzing your concept instantly to see if the logic holds up.

How It Works

It's very simple to use - You describe your idea, and our AI evaluates it across three simple pillars:

  • The Problem: Is the pain point real and worth solving?
  • Concept & USP: Does your solution have a unique edge in the market?
  • The Verdict: A straightforward score to help you decide if it’s time to move forward.
Our Goal

Our goal is to help you find out if your business idea has potential. This tool will highlight the best parts of your idea, identify potential challenges that you may face, and suggest extra feature and benefit ideas you can add to help you move forward with confidence.

"Is your business idea worth pursuing? Find out with AffiStrike."
