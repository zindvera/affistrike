Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026
Zero Data Collection

At AffiStrike, we take a "Privacy-First" approach. Because our platform is built as a frontend-only application using a Cloudflare proxy and the Groq API, we do not have a backend database to store your personal information or your business ideas.

Any text you enter into the "Analyze Idea" box is processed in real-time. We do not save, store, or log the content of your ideas. Once you refresh or close the page, the data is gone from our end.

Technical Processing

To provide our AI evaluation, your input is sent through a Cloudflare proxy server to the Groq API.

  • Transience: Data is processed "in-flight" and is not retained by AffiStrike after the response is generated.
  • Security: All communication between your browser and our processing layers is encrypted via HTTPS.
Third-Party Links

Our evaluation results may contain affiliate links to external tools (e.g., Shopify, Canva). Please note that once you leave AffiStrike, you are subject to the privacy policies of those specific third-party websites.

Contact Us

If you choose to email us at mockoverflow@gmail.com, we will only use your email address to reply to your specific inquiry.
