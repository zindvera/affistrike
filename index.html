1. What is AffiStrike?

AffiStrike is an AI-powered "gut check" that tells you if your business idea is worth pursuing before you spend time or money building it.

Most entrepreneurs ask friends for feedback, but friends are often too nice to tell the truth. AffiStrike acts as your digital sounding board, giving you a completely objective and instant analysis of your concept.

It evaluates your idea across three pillars: the reality of the problem you are trying to solve, the USP of your idea, and finally, the verdict score on whether your idea is worth pursuing or if it needs improvement. You will come to know the greatest strengths of your idea, the problems you might face, and the steps you need to take to move ahead with your business idea.

Understanding Your Results

After you submit your idea, our AI breaks down your concept into three specific evaluation cards. This isn't just a generic guess; it's an analysis of how your idea fits into the real world:

  • • The Problem (Market Need) Everything starts with the problem. We look at the pain point you identified to see if it’s a real, "burning" issue that people are willing to pay to solve. A high score here means you’ve found a gap in the market that actually needs filling.
  • • Concept & USP (Your Solution) This card evaluates the logic of your solution. We want to see your "Unique Selling Proposition" - the edge that makes your idea different from everything else out there. If your idea is too similar to everything else, well, you might want to see where you need to be more original.
  • • Should You Start (The Verdict) This is your weighted score, the result of combining the strength of the problem with the originality of your solution. It’s the final evaluation that will tell you whether you should start now, or if you should take some time to work on your idea.

Your Strategic Roadmap (Improve My Idea)

If you choose to use the "Improve My Idea" feature, you'll unlock a deeper layer of advice. This is where we help you move from a simple concept to an actual launch plan:

  • 1. Features & Benefits Tips We offer you ideas for additional features or benefits you may not have considered. These are intended to increase the utility of your product for your customers and make your product different and more useful than your competitor's product or service.
  • 2. Strategic Marketing Ideas Your toughest challenge will be acquiring your first 10 customers, and we can help you with a variety of free "organic" methods, as well as paid advertising ideas, to get you off the ground quickly without wasting money on advertising that does not produce results.
